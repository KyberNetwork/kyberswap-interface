# PancakeSwap Liquidity Widgets Package

PancakeSwap-specific Zap In widget for PancakeSwap V3 pools. Fork/adaptation of `@kyberswap/liquidity-widgets` with PancakeSwap-specific contract integrations and branding.

## Main Export

`PancakeLiquidityWidget` from `@kyberswap/pancake-liquidity-widgets`

## Supported Chains

- BNB Smart Chain (56) - Primary
- Ethereum (1) - Secondary
